# User interview: Sarah, Head of Ops at Harbor Logistics

**Interviewer:** Priya (PM)
**Date:** April 18
**Duration:** 45 min (excerpted)

---

**Priya:** Thanks for making time, Sarah. I want to walk through how you use
the platform week-to-week. Start wherever feels natural.

**Sarah:** ok. um. so mondays are the worst, honestly. i come in and i have
to pull three reports before our 10am stand-up — shipments-by-region,
carrier-cost-variance, and the one with the on-time delivery thing, i always
forget the name. and every week i have to rebuild the filters. every. single.
week. like i know it's a small thing but it adds up to maybe 15, 20 minutes
before i've even had coffee.

**Priya:** mm.

**Sarah:** and i've asked the team if there's a way to save them and everyone
says "just bookmark the url" but the filter state isn't in the url, so that
doesn't work, and — yeah. that's a big one.

**Priya:** is that the most painful part of mondays, or are there others?

**Sarah:** oh there's others. the csv export — when i pull more than 10,000
rows it just... stops. no warning. for the longest time i thought our volume
was down and actually the export was silently cut off. i caught it like three
weeks in when the finance numbers didn't match. we had to restate two weekly
reports. it was bad. your support team was great about it but it was a mess.

**Priya:** yeah i heard about that one from the support side.

**Sarah:** yeah. so now i don't trust the export at all, honestly. i do a
rough sanity check every time — i pull a count from the dashboard first and
compare. adds another five minutes.

**Priya:** got it.

**Sarah:** um. what else. the mobile experience is really bad. but i don't
think i'm your mobile persona? like i'm at a desk 90% of the time. when i'm
at the warehouse once a week it would be nice to pull up the on-time number
on my phone and the current view is like, not usable. but — i don't think
that's your biggest priority. just mentioning.

**Priya:** no, good to know.

**Sarah:** the other thing is — and this is a weird one — we have two other
finance people who need some of these reports too but the role system is
all-or-nothing. either they can see everything, including some stuff they
shouldn't, or nothing. so i've been manually forwarding csvs to them, which
is obviously terrible. but there's no good in-between. i wouldn't even know
what to ask for — like — shared saved views with read-only access? i don't
know.

**Priya:** that's helpful. do you remember roughly when this started being a
pain? was it always this way, or —

**Sarah:** no, honestly it got worse when we grew. we went from 40 shipments
a day to 150 or so over the last 18 months and the workflows just don't
scale. like, the export thing was invisible when we had 3000 rows a week. the
monday thing was annoying at 40 shipments, it's a real problem at 150.

**Priya:** makes sense. if i could wave a wand and fix one of these for you
tomorrow, which one?

**Sarah:** the saved filters thing. because i deal with it every single week.
the export thing scared me but i have a workaround now, i check every time.
the role thing i can limp along with manual forwarding. but the filters i
literally can't work around. it's just 20 minutes of my life every monday.

**Priya:** got it. anything else you want to flag before we wrap?

**Sarah:** no i think that's — oh, one more. i would pay for a scheduled
version of these reports landing in my inbox monday at 7am so i don't have
to log in at all. but i know that's a whole other thing.

**Priya:** noted. thanks sarah, this was super helpful.
